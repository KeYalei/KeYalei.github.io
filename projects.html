Physical activity, sedentary behavior and risks of diseases in China: a PheWAS analysis of CKB data

Physical inactivity has been identified as a major risk factor for several major chronic diseases including cardiometabolic diseases and a few cancers, but it's understudied in the Chinese population.

COPD and all-cause and cause-specific mortality in China

The burden of chronic obstructive pulmonary disease (COPD) in China has been increasingly recognized, yet a significant gap in public understanding persists. However, the mortality associated with COPD is often underestimated due to underdiagnosis.

An Exploratory Study of Using ECG to Predict Diseases and Laboratory Measurements

This project aims to leverage the extensive CKB Electrocardiogram (ECG) data to extract thousands of ECG digital biomarkers using the ECG Founder model. Key biomarkers of particular interest include blood potassium levels and cardiac age.
